Our Company

TLCC MEDICAL HEALTH SERVICES aims to provide utmost care for every member of the family, from babies to adults and to companies from staff to management. Our team of professional doctors ensures that every individual recieves our utmost care.

We ensure compliance to public health (DOH Accredited), environmental laws and regulation (DENR and CENRO), to be able to serve you with commitment to improve lives in the community we live in by promoting, educating and creating health consciousness and establish safe and healthy environment and workplaces.

The combined expertise of our doctors, medical professionals and partners are geared in serving you not only to give you just a healthcare but the Tender Loving Care that everyone deserves

Vision

To promote, educate, and create a commnunity with utmost life care and to establish a workplace that is drug-free, healthy and fit for a better life and a great future ahead.

Mission

To make every individual healthy, fit and drug-free with the best of care from within to make each life worth living.

Objective
  • To render service that is cost effective for the basic medical need of an individual.
  • To guide people with disease/s with proper education and knowledge.
  • To provide assistance in making a community and workplaces that is drug-free.
